Features

OEDE proposes practical functions that can help transform distributed educational practices into connected, reusable, and sustainable learning assets.

📚

Multi-textbook Blocks

Learning blocks can draw from multiple textbooks and perspectives.

👩‍🏫

Teacher Design Network

Teachers share, revise, and accumulate instructional design experiences.

🤖

AI Learning Support

AI recommends pathways while learners and teachers remain active decision-makers.

🧭

Mentor Network

University students, retired teachers, and community experts can support learners.

🪪

Credential Mobility

Learning records support continuity across schools and regions.

🌍

Global Learning

Multilingual and multicultural access supports global educational belonging.
