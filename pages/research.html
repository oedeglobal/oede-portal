Research Direction

This portal supports the OEDE research trajectory from Korean academic publication toward international discussion and future global educational collaboration.

KSET Paper

The current paper presents OEDE as an Open Educational Design Ecosystem that connects and accumulates distributed instructional design experiences.

International Expansion

Future work may develop OEDE for EdMedia, AACE, EC-TEL, and UNESCO-related discussions, including learning recovery, mobility, and multilingual support.

Digital Silk Road of Learning

The Digital Silk Road concept frames OEDE as a pathway for connecting educational assets across cultures, languages, and life circumstances.

Learning Recovery Protocol

Future work may examine learning-first pathways in which learners can begin learning before formal recognition is fully completed, especially in disruption contexts.
