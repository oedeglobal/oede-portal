7-Layer Architecture

The OEDE architecture is organized into seven connected layers that describe how educational resources, human expertise, learner participation, and global mobility can form a coherent ecosystem.

01

AI-Driven Adaptive Learning Content

Connects multiple textbooks, learning blocks, and AI-assisted recommendations while preserving diverse educational perspectives.

02

Distributed Instructional Design

Links teacher-generated lessons, assessments, explanations, and mentoring experiences as shared design assets.

03

Multi-Level Learning Analytics

Analyzes learning data at learner, class, school, regional, and system levels to support reflective decision-making.

04

Learner-in-the-loop

Allows learners to negotiate, adjust, and reflect on AI-recommended pathways with teachers and mentors.

05

Multicultural & Educational Welfare Integration

Connects learning support with language, welfare, counseling, and community resources for diverse learners.

06

Learning Credential & Mobility

Supports portable learning records and continuity during school transfer, migration, overseas stay, or educational disruption.

07

Global Learning Layer

Links multilingual resources, global educator networks, diaspora learners, and cross-cultural learning opportunities.
