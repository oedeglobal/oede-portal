About OEDE

Open Educational Design Ecosystem is a conceptual framework for connecting, accumulating, and reusing distributed educational design experiences.

Core Problem

The central issue is not simply the lack of educational materials. The more fundamental challenge is that valuable teaching experiences, learning resources, and practical instructional designs are often disconnected and lost over time.

Core Idea

OEDE proposes that educational assets should be connected, accumulated, adapted, and sustained as a living ecosystem. Teachers, mentors, learners, families, welfare services, and AI all participate in coordinated support.

Research Orientation

OEDE does not frame existing schools, textbooks, or platforms as failures. Instead, it complements existing systems by connecting distributed expertise and supporting learning continuity across schools, regions, countries, and life circumstances.
