Prototype Gallery

Readable dashboard images illustrate how OEDE can be experienced by learners, teachers, administrators, mentors, and global coordinators. Click each image to enlarge.

학습 공동체 연결

Learner Community Connection

OEDE connects learners, teachers, mentors, and learning resources into a shared learning space. This reflects the core idea that educational design experiences should not remain isolated but should become reusable and connected assets.

학생·교사·관리자 공간

Learner, Teacher, and Administrator Views

The platform model includes learner-facing spaces and administrator dashboards so that individual learning support and institutional coordination can operate together.

학습자료 및 교수자 조합

Learning Resource and Instructor Combination

Learners can combine textbook blocks, teacher explanations, mentor support, and AI-generated guidance. This supports learning pathways that are negotiated rather than fixed.

분산형 학습 공간

Distributed Learning Space

The distributed learning layer shows how multiple textbooks, teachers, and learning materials can be connected so that educational design experience becomes a shared resource.

인간-AI 협력 학습 지원

Human-AI Collaborative Support

The learner is placed at the center while AI, teachers, mentors, peers, and family form a coordinated support network. AI is treated as one supporter among many, not as a replacement for human education.

다양한 경로의 수학 학습

Multiple Pathways for Concept Learning

A single concept can be learned through diverse explanations, representations, and cultural examples. This supports multilingual and multicultural understanding.

수학 학습 분석 대시보드

Learning Analytics and Self-Perception

The dashboard compares AI-estimated achievement, learner self-perception, mentor feedback, and learning evidence. The gap between objective performance and subjective perception becomes a meaningful learning signal.

학습 이동 및 연속성

Learning Mobility and Continuity

OEDE supports learners who move between schools, regions, or countries by maintaining learning history and connecting the next learning pathway.

학습 인증 및 이동성 센터

Learning Credential and Mobility Center

Learning records, achievement evidence, and credentials can be managed in a portable form so that learning continuity is not limited by one institution.

교육복지 연계 및 다문화 지원

Educational Welfare and Multicultural Support

This layer connects translation, mentoring, welfare services, and learning support for multicultural learners and educationally vulnerable students.

전세계 학습 이동 대시보드

Global Learning Mobility and Asset Linkage

The dashboard represents the movement of learners and educational assets across countries and regions, supporting learning continuity beyond local boundaries.

글로벌 교육설계 확장

Global Instructional Design Expansion

OEDE is visualized as a global instructional design ecosystem connecting regional managers, learning assets, institutional support, and community participation.

글로벌 학습 연속성 지도

Digital Silk Road of Learning

The global map expresses OEDE as a Digital Silk Road of Learning, where educational assets and learner support can move across places and circumstances.

글로벌 학습 연속성 지도 보조

Global Continuity Alternative View

This alternative view emphasizes educational asset circulation and learner mobility within a broader international support network.

글로벌 학습 연속성 세부

Detailed Global Continuity View

This view illustrates how learning records, mentoring, welfare services, and institutional pathways can be coordinated across contexts.
