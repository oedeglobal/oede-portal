Welcome to OEDE

OEDE is a global research portal for connecting, sharing, and accumulating educational design experiences. It supports learner-centered education, learning continuity, multicultural understanding, and global educational collaboration.

ConnectConnect educators, learners, mentors, schools, and educational resources across contexts.
AccumulatePreserve valuable educational design experiences as reusable educational assets.
PersonalizeSupport learner-centered pathways through AI-assisted recommendations and human guidance.
AnalyzeUse learning analytics to compare performance, self-perception, and support needs.
CollaborateEnable teachers, mentors, welfare services, and communities to coordinate learning support.
SustainSupport learning continuity across regions, schools, countries, and life circumstances.

About OEDE

OEDE reframes future education as an ecosystem that connects distributed instructional design experiences rather than merely producing more digital content.

학습 공동체 연결

Learner Community Connection

OEDE connects learners, teachers, mentors, and learning resources into a shared learning space. This reflects the core idea that educational design experiences should not remain isolated but should become reusable and connected assets.

학생·교사·관리자 공간

Learner, Teacher, and Administrator Views

The platform model includes learner-facing spaces and administrator dashboards so that individual learning support and institutional coordination can operate together.

학습자료 및 교수자 조합

Learning Resource and Instructor Combination

Learners can combine textbook blocks, teacher explanations, mentor support, and AI-generated guidance. This supports learning pathways that are negotiated rather than fixed.

분산형 학습 공간

Distributed Learning Space

The distributed learning layer shows how multiple textbooks, teachers, and learning materials can be connected so that educational design experience becomes a shared resource.

Multilingual Access

OEDE is designed for multilingual and multicultural learning contexts. Language pages are prepared for Korean, English, Spanish, Arabic, Chinese, German, French, Japanese, and Afrikaans.
