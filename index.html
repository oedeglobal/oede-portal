Welcome to OEDE

About OEDE

OEDE is a conceptual framework proposed by Junghoon Lee that integrates technology, pedagogy, and human educational values. Its central concern is not simply to produce more content, but to connect and accumulate distributed educational design experiences generated in real educational practice.

The framework aims to support learning continuity by linking AI-assisted content, teacher-generated instructional design, learning analytics, learner participation, educational welfare, learning credential mobility, and global learning networks.
